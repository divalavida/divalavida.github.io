Product Management

I build products because I love the challenge of combining communication and technical knowledge to get things done.

I think good products should always be: Clean, efficiently approached, logical to the user, attention grabbing, interaction guiding, and should always make a point.

Here are examples of my work:

Space by Tinybop

Space is an app that provides an educational exploration of the solar system, rocket ships, and planets. As lead Product Manager, I facilitated ideation, technical development, and it's release. Space can be found on display devices all Apple stores worldwide.

MaestroConference

Maestro Conference is a Conferencing system that provides various tools to improve collaboration in conferencing. I PM'ed the creation of a beta tool that would allow users to vote on concepts and topics remotely through the conferencing platform.

Creatures by Tinybop

IN PROGRESS: Creatures is an interactive iOS app that promotes trial and error learning by allowing kids to build and test their own creatures. I managed all early aspect of app creation.
